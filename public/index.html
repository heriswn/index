The Importance of Privacy

Online privacy is important because it helps protect personal privacy, prevent cybercrime, defend freedom of expression, and safeguard trust. For example, if we do not protect our online privacy, others can easily access our personal information such as our name, address and telephone number, which can lead to misuse of this information and threaten our personal security. In addition, if we do not protect our online privacy, other parties can access sensitive information such as passwords and credit card numbers, which can result in cybercrimes such as phishing and identity theft.

Philanthropically I focus on personal privacy. Did you know that your digital track record can be known by several parties, and take advantage of it?
